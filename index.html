Ameri D. Purple

Main Protagonist in The Talking Raven novel

Ameri D. Purple is the main protagonist of the novel The Talking Raven by Jeni D. Gorobchuk. At the beginning of the novel, Ameri was portrayed as a weak girl who was barely clinging on to life until she found a special friend that taught her how to be strong. As the story goes on she started learning new skills and started accepting her circumstances and fight for her life. Her special friend is a raven who refers to himself as Mr. Crow. It is a talking crow whom only Ameri could hear and understand. Mr. Raven has abilities to tell calamities that are about to happen a week before it happens. Ameri and Mr. Raven traveled the world together for many years and become closer more than ever. Ameri loves grilled spicy wings, desserts, fruits and seafoods. She hates all types of vegetables and she develop a magic where she can obtain chicken plant that produces eggs as fruits, and once ripens, can crack into chicks. She also obtain a skill where she can program a parallel world to her liking and rule over it using a magical gadget she have invented.
